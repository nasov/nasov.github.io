A short bio:

 

 

 

 

 

Nasov explores the traditional subjects of folk music, such as mammals, vegetables, body secretions. After four years spent in London, where he composed the songs that constitute the backbone of his body of work, he moved to back to his home town in Italy, crafting his mediocrity. His music style has been described as a blend of indie pop and alt-folk played by a very talented eight year old.
Since 2015, he has made a name in the Berlin music scene thanks to his average talent and his unexceptional character.
